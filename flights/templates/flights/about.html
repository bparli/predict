About The Flights Mystic

With the power of The Flights Mystic, air travelers can now foresee airline and aiport delays and cancellations. Using Bayesian Inference with historical On-Time Performance data collected by the Bureau of Transportation Statistics, The Flights Mystic can help you plan your air travel and maximize your valuable time.
